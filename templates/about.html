Meet Chef Joseph Graham

Owner of @toomuchsawcecateringLLC. God-fearing, football fanatic, music lover — bringing hospitality and heart to every plate.

“Food is a love language. We listen first, then we cook.”

Chef Joseph Graham built Too Much Sawce on family recipes, relentless curiosity, and the belief that great service matters as much as great flavor. His journey runs Tuskegee → Huntsville → Denver → Indianapolis → Charlotte, and those miles show up in the menu — Southern comfort, Latin and coastal influences, and elegant plating that still feels like home.
